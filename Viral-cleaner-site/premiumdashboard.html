Step-by-Step Premium Cleaning (Monthly)

  1. Update and run a FULL Malwarebytes scan.
  2. Open CCleaner → run Health Check & full junk cleanup.
  3. Review startup apps again, disable new slow apps.
  4. Clear browser cache/history in all browsers.
  5. Check for Windows Update security patches.
  6. Remove large unused files (downloads, videos, installers).
  7. Uninstall unused programs or suspicious apps.
  8. Restart your device for performance boost.
  9. Run one more quick Malwarebytes scan after restart.
  10. Record results in your monthly notes (below).
